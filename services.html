Services

Chartering

JUST BULK is a dry cargo company specializing in worldwide chartering within Handy to Capesize markets. We provide dry bulk shipping services for global coal, grain, iron ore and fertilizer traders, end users and shippers. Our chartering activities cover spot and period chartering and contracts of affreightment for charterers, operators and owners, handling both competitive and exclusive business.

Our team has a dept market knowledge and chartered vessels to the international trading houses of dry bulk commodities, world famous vessel operators that span across five continents. We make management of the entire supply chain from origin to destination.

Brokerage

Next to our direct dry bulk chartering services, we offer a wide range of vessel brokerage services for all your bulk cargo needs around the globe.

JUST BULK can offer full market information on the dry bulk chartering with background particulars due to their extensive database containing worldwide controlled tonnage.

Also supportive to the traditional shipbroking services we are affording free research services to our clients, considering the shipowners viewpoint choosing the right vessels for the certain cargoes and trades, as well as all the special requirements of the different commodities to be transported. With quality maintenance programmed in place, we warrant that our customers achieve their business goals.

Ship Management

JUST BULK provides a full commercial and technical management services, as well as recruitment of crew for various types of dry cargo ships. We work with any contract type (CP, COA, TC, MOA), ensuring safe and the most advantageous conditions of duties for the Shipowners.

Our experienced marine and technical superintendents provide full support to the vessel’s Master in the planning and execution of voyages.

We instruct the Master and further support in all commercial issues related to the terms and conditions of the transportation contract, keep in touch with perspective weather forecasts, coordination of a safe and speedy transition route with him.

JB fully complies with International Safety Management (ISM) requirements to ensure safe and efficient vessel operations. We serve our clients in full post-fixing (including Demurrage, Dispatch calculations, invoicing and accounts, recording and tracking of the Shipowner’s funds).

Technical Department oversees and supports relevant operations including all kinds of scheduled and unplanned services, dock and voyage repair, preparing all technical aspects of the vessel in accordance with particular tasks to ensure the most efficient commercial operation.
